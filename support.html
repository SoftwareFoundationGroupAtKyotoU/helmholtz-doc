Instruction support status¶

Summary¶

  • MAP instruction is not supported.

  • Bitwise operation is not supported.

  • Types and related instructions of never, ticket, bls12_381_g1, bls12_381_g2, bls12_381_fr, sapling_transaction, and sapling_state are not supported.
